Food groups that are considered Carbohydrates:
Beans, grains, pastas, breads
Starchy vegetables
Dairy products, milk alternatives
Fruits
Snacks and baked goods
Condiments, salad dressing
MY PLATE RATIO AMOUNTS
Vegetables: Make half your plate non-starchy vegetables. Try fresh, frozen or low-sodium canned.
Protein: Examples include chicken, turkey, lean cuts of beef, eggs, nuts, fish and tuna. Make sure that these meats are baked, boiled, sautéed, rotisserie, grilled instead of fried.
Carbohydrates: Consume recommended carbohydrate amounts as directed, always chose whole grain products.
Remember eat 3 meals and 3 snacks (add a protein) on a schedule try to eat at the same times everyday!***
